What is Flava Base?

Bringing the Taste of Punjab to Your Event

Flava Base is a modern Punjabi street food catering brand serving wraps, curry boxes, loaded fries, noodles and Punjabi street food starters for weddings, corporate events, private celebrations and pop-ups.
