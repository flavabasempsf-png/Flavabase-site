Our Story

From Mum's Kitchen
to the Streets

Flava Base is inspired by recipes from my mum's kitchen, brought to life with a modern Punjabi street food twist.

The flavours, spices and techniques behind every dish come from real home cooking — bold, authentic and full of flavour, using fresh, locally sourced ingredients wherever possible.

Food has always been my connection to home, and Flava Base is built in memory of my mum — keeping those flavours and that feeling alive.
